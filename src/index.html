African Education Summit 2023

"Empowering Minds, Transforming Futures"

Welcome to the African Education Summit 2023, a gathering of visionary leaders, educators, and experts dedicated to advancing education across the African continent. Join us for an inspiring two-day event where we will explore innovative solutions, share success stories, and forge partnerships to unlock the immense potential of African education.

2023.06.05(MON) ~ 2023.06.08(THU)

@ National Theater of Karaa, Nairobi, Kenya.

Main Program

  • Talks

    Listen to inspiring speakers from various countries about their experience in African Education.

  • Exhibition

    Appreciate cutting edge approaches currently being experimented with all thought the African continent.

  • Forum

    Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions with experts for each topic.

  • Workshop

    Experience first hand how lessons are delivered to students, rather than simply listening talks about it.

  • Ignite

    Get opportunities to network with affiliates and experts around the continent.
